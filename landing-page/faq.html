Frequently Asked Questions

Chances are, somebody has already had the same question that you have.

How do I post a new job?
To post a new job, you must have an account, which is free. Login to your account, click on the right hand navigation icon and tap 'Create Job'. Make sure you fill all the fields.

What is workandrise.com?
workandrise.com serves as a job marketplace to empower the people of Uganda to elevate their station in life. Employers post jobs, then hire & make payments on the platform - including an escrow payment to Work&Rise to facilitate trust between employer and freelancer. If the work was not completed in a satisfactory way, the service provider will not be paid! It does not matter which part of the world you are coming from. All you need is a computer and internet connection to start benefiting.

How do I Join workandrise.com?
Signing up to our platform is very easy and free. Click here and fill the short form with your details. Follow the link in your email and confirm your address.

How can I trust workandrise.com?
Our system is designed to ensure that you are fully protected 24/7. Work rest assured that you are safe and protected. Our Payment Protection assures you that you pay only for work you've approved. Should you have an issue with a service provider, we have systems to help you. Should a freelancer have an issue with an employer not fulfilling the terms of their agreement, we have systems to help you.

How do I make the payment to a service provider?
You pay your service providers on an hourly basis, and determine the number of hours you are looking for work in the job posting. For each payment you make to the service provider, we deduct 10%. For example, if you pay your service provider US$10, they earn US$9 and we receive US$1.

How do I set up a payment method?
Once an employer approves a job application, they will then receive a notification to fund the Work&Rise escrow through their MobileMoney account.

How does a service provider get paid?
When the employer certifies the completion of the work, the freelancer will almost instantly receive their payment through the MobileMoney linked phone number that they provide to Work&Rise.

What kind of work can service providers at workandrise.com do?
Any kind of work that can be done on a computer or in the physical worl can be done through our platform. The service providers in our database are able to execute a variety of assignments – big or small, one-off or repeat, individual or team-based.

What if I’m not happy with the results?
Most projects on our platform get completed on-time and as expected. If there’s a disagreement between you and your service provider, we’ll provide free dispute assistance and, if needed, connect you with arbitration. We have a network of arbitration service providers available to help you. Some fees may be applicable when arbitration service providers are engaged.

How do I protect my Intellectual Property?
The Service Contract terms in our Terms and Conditions set forth confidentiality obligations and your right to ownership of intellectual property. You can also add additional terms to your job, and you can have your service provider sign an additional Non-Disclosure Agreement if needed. Who owns the legal rights to work product developed by a service provider engaged on workandrise.com? Upon payment for work completed, our Terms of Service specify that ownership of that work transfers to the Client. If desired, service providers and Clients may also agree on different or additional terms regarding work product ownership. For details, see our terms and conditions of service.

How does workandrise.com make money?
We charge your service provider a 10% service fee on payments. For example, if you and your service provider agree to a US$100 price for a job, when you send US$100 through our payment system, we deduct US$10 (10%) and release the remaining US$90 to your service provider. We also make money by offering advertising space on our platform.
