Building Your Profile is a Process

1. What other Users See about You
Our marketplace is made of diverse experts, agencies and companies from all around the world. Your identity is important in this market place. Others should be able to verify the information which you provide. To maintain identity, the following is critical: * A user cannot have more than one account. At signing up you decide whether you are a client or a service provider but you cannot be both. Please note that accounts should not be shared or duplicated in any way. * If you’re to use a logo, it should be appropriate for a professional, global community. * Your profile should not mislead or falsely imply a relationship with another company or person. 2. Job Posts
If you are ever going to benefit fully from our community, you need to make sure that your job posts are written clearly, precisely and in English. Any language other than English is prohibited. All job posts must abide by the following policies: * No one is allowed to ask for contact information from members of our community before contract is entered into. * Jobs must be free of offensive language and advertisements. * Posting the same job multiple times is prohibited. * Requesting a service that is illegal or violates intellectual property rights, copyrights or the terms of service of another service, product or website is prohibited. * Requesting free work of any kind is prohibited. * Requesting a fee in order to apply or bid is strictly prohibited. All transactions are free until a contract is entered into. * Jobs may not transmit any content related to or containing any adult or sexually explicit material. * Requesting the creation of content that violates academic integrity is prohibited. * Jobs must be free of spammy content. 3. Soliciting for Payments outside workandrise.com
Soliciting or processing payment outside of workandrise.com is prohibited for the safety and security of all our users. If a service provider solicits work to be done and paid for outside of the workandrise.com, please report them immediately. 4. Feedback
Honesty and objectivity are two very important values in our community. When you give feedback, be as honest and factual as possible as this will help others in their relationships with the service provider or client. Please note that in some cases we may delete feedback found to violate our Terms of Service. Attempts to falsify feedback, manipulate, or coerce another user by threatening negative feedback are strictly prohibited, as is any offer to sell or buy services in exchange for positive feedback. Feedback containing the following types of content may be deleted: * Spam, advertising or other commercial content * Content that endorses illegal activity * Content in a language other than English * Content that personally identifies another user * Profane, vulgar, obscene, slanderous, libelous, threatening or discriminatory language, or adult material
