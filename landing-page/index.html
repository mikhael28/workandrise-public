Economic Empowerment for Uganda

Work and Rise connects Ugandans who are looking for part-time, freelance work to employers that are looking for flexible arrangements.


21st Century
Ugandan Business

You may have a full-time job, though chances are you're looking for extra cash on the side. Work & Rise helps freelancers search for jobs & gigs in their local markets. Freelancers create a profile page for themselves, listing their skills and qualifications - from there, they apply for jobs that are posted online and can discuss the terms of work.

PesaMoni Integration

Work & Rise integrates with PesaMoni - a Kenyan payment-processing startup that lets you to transfer your earnings into MobileMoney or MPesa instantly. Our two companies together symbolize the friendship betweent the people of Uganda & Kenya.

verified_user

Trust & Verify

After a job is agreed upon, Employers deposit funds from their PesaMoni into a escrow account. Once the work is verified as complete, the payments are released into the Freelancer's Pesamoni wallet.

fingerprint

Security & Privacy

Your privacy and security are top concerns - we encrypt traffic through HTTPS, offer two-factor authentication, & a number of cyber-security countermeasures to protect your data. We will never sell your data to third parties, and will always work to harden our security infrastructure.
