MobileMoney & Mpesa Integration

Work & Rise integrates with MobileMoney & MPesa through MTN, Airtel & UTL - helping employers pay freelancers quickly and conveniently on our platform.

Trust & Verify

After a job is agreed upon, Employers deposit funds into the Work&Rise escrow account. Once the work is verified as complete, the payments are released into the Freelancer's MobileMoney wallet.

Security & Privacy

Your privacy and security are top concerns - we encrypt traffic through HTTPS, secure our API's, & offer a number of cyber-security countermeasures to protect your data. We will never sell your data to third parties, and will always work to harden our security infrastructure.
